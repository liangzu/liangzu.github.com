About Me (CV)

Since 2021 I have been a PhD student at the Johns Hopkins University, supervised by Professor René Vidal. I was a master student at ShanghaiTech, advised by Professor Manolis. I did my undergraduate at Zhejiang University.

I seek mathematical or algorithmic answers to the questions that arise in machine learning, computer vision, signal processing. The problems of interest to me include (but are not limited to)

1. homomorphic sensing (1 2 3)
2. linear regression without correspondences (1 2)
3. geometric vision
4. matrix completion (1)
5. robust (unlabeled) PCA (1)

I am using tools from algebraic geometry, optimization, and probability to study geometric computer vision problems.
