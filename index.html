About Me (CV)

I am a third-year graduate student in SIST@ShanghaiTech University. My advisor is Manolis C. Tsakiris. Previously, I did my undergraduate in Zhejiang University.

My interest is in mathematics of data science. This involves learning from data a certain model which could be geometric, algebraic, combinatorial, or statistical. To promote theoretical understanding of the model, it often demands tools from algebraic geometry, high-dimensional probability, and optimization theory. At the same time, I am also interested in developing algorithms for (3D) computer vision. See also my research statement.

I am conducting research into homomorphic sensing using algebraic-geometric methods.
