Online courses

The below two courses contain different topics and complement each other.

CS184.1x on xuetangX
This online course from my point of view is compact, self-contained and a little bit hard(due to its compactness). It will teach you the basic graphics pipeline and how graphics programs work.
CS184 at UC Berkeley
This course compared to previous one is much more substantial, the slides on the website are extremely clear and thus deverve your attentions.

btw, I finished only 50% of each… I am no longer a graphics guy. :P
