Learning How to Learn

Learning via official docs, stack overflow, google, etc.

Advice

Never try to read a book from the beginning to the end

Because it will not make you from zero to hero. Instead, scanning the table of the contents, and carefully reading the preface (and the first few chapters if necessary), which will give you a sense that what can you learn from this book.

books as a tool
When you are tackling your problems and you are confused about something(e.g., the syntax of python), you then consult google/your books or anything that can help you. If you remember the contents of books roughly, then you can know which knowledge(the answer of the confusing problems) is in which book.
MOOC is your road
there are many great MOOCs on the web, which actually ease the pain of learning. you as a modern IT student should always find appropriate MOOCs first when you need to learn something new.
