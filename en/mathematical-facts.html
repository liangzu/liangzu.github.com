1 Matrix Calculus

  • The gradient of \(f(b)=a^Tb\) w.r.t \(b\) is \(a\),
  • The gradient of \(f(a)=a^TXa\) w.r.t. \(a\) is \(Xa\). (hint: \(a^TXa=\Sigma_{i,j}x_{ij}a_ia_j\).)
  • The gradient of \(f(x)=Ax\) is \(A\).
