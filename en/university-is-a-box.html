Introduction

Have you ever remembered the little prince? He ask 'me' a sheep, and finally, 'I' got impatient, drawing him a box.

Today that all I want to say is: University is also, and is only a box.

To realize the university(U for short) is really a box, there are three stages we need to walk through. Let's go!

The first stage: full box

Just like many other students graduating from high school, I believed a myth that U are a beautiful box full of all the things I had longed for: friendship, freedom, respect and love. U would give me what I want and I could take anything out.

I felt lost in the first two years and missed many opportunities, I did much terrible jobs and didn't give something honest efforts. One day, I run on the playground, cried, and murmured:"What's … going … on?". I used to be active and enthusiastic. What happened to me?

I heard some voices, from the heaven or from the hell:

…U are a difficult case…

I screamed out to the University:"What's U give me? and HOW!". I opened the box and found … "Are U university kidding?"

I heard some voices, from the heaven or from the hell:

…Why?…

The second stage: circuit box

SRTP project opened me a door and leaded me to the research. I pretend to be a grown-up and talk about system with them.

Yes, grown-ups always like to talk about systems:

  • U are a system, or just like a circuit, aiming to achieve "The same INPUT, the better OUTPUT!"(comparing to the other "circuit").

I heard some voices, from the heaven or from the hell:

…The individual is only a cell…

"No, they are denying the individual." I said, "No, I refuse to enter into this circuit."

I entered.

I met the simplest flip-flop, I saw the register defending its own status, I was blocked by the multiplexer, I got trapped.

There seemed no road to "the better OUTPUT". Were there any way turning back?

I heard some voices, from the heaven or from the hell:

…no RE, and no SEARCH…

…U…don't…exist…

The third stage: empty box

Conclusion

In the first stage, I was out of the box because I believed that the box was full of what I wanted; In the second stage, I dived into the box to find my road; In the third stage, .

Full of anything? No, there isn't.

The road to the better output? No, there isn't.

The grown-ups emphasize the value of U, ignoring the value of me(students).

How could circuits work without the knowledge of the INPUT?

U just an empty box. We must put something in before we can take anything out. There is no friendship in U; friendship is in students, and students put it into U. We must learn the art and form the habit of giving, loving, serving, praising to keep the box full.

Or, U are another empty box. There is no circuits and no systems. We must emphasize the ability and the existence of ourself and jump out of the box.

I heard some voices, from the heaven or from the hell:

…I saw U and U saw me…
