Liangzu Peng is currently a PhD student at Johns Hopkins University, supervised by Rene Vidal. He has research interests in mathematics and algorithms of data science and their applications to problems that arise in machine learning, geometric computer vision, and robotics. He received his Master degree from ShanghaiTech University under the supervision of Manolis C. Tsakiris in 2021 and had doing his undergraduate study at Zhejiang University from 2013 to 2017.
